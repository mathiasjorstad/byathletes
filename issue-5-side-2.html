McGregor's Proper No. Twelve Gets €50M Distillery Expansion

April 7, 2026

Conor McGregor's Proper No. Twelve Irish whiskey brand — which he sold a majority stake in to Becle for $600M in 2021 — is investing €50M in a new County Kildare distillery set to open in 2027. McGregor retains a minority equity stake and remains active on brand strategy.
