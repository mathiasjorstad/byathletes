Your Name Gets You the First Meeting. Your Operator Gets You the Second.

April 7, 2026

Too many athlete-founded companies are stuck at Series A because the founder is the entire commercial team. The ones that scale — SpringHill, Proper No. Twelve, Kings League — all hired a professional operator within 18 months of launch. Your equity is the asset. Hire someone to protect it.
